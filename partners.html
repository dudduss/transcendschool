We provide the highest quality recruiting service to grow your mortgage teams

There are several options out there to hire new employees, but we believe that Transcend is the most convenient and will provide the highest return on investment. Here's why:

High Quality Pipeline

You will have access to a growing pipeline of trainees that are motivated, diligent, and have relevant experience. Save time sourcing and evaluating these qualities during your recruiting processes.

Day 1 Ready

Our curriculum is comprehensive and practical, making our trainees the most job ready. Whether you provide leads or need employees to generate their own leads, you'll save time and money bringing new hires up to speed.

Diversity

Our trainees come from a variety of backgrounds and will bring fresh perspectives and ideas to your company. This will help your company's business grow faster and also improve employee retention.

At a much more reasonable cost than traditional recruiting methods

We only charge you when you decide to hire one of our trainees. Our payment plan is lower cost and lower risk, ensuring that your recruiting dollars are actually delivering you value. Here's the cost breakdown:

Security Deposit

We charge a $5,000 deposit once you've hired a Transcend trainee. We will return that deposit to you if the trainee does not work at your company for at least 6 months (either they leave or you let them go).

Placement Fee

We charge a placement fee of 15% of the employee's first year salary at their one year anniversary. If they leave before then, we charge 15% of salary earned before their departure. Your security deposit will go towards covering this placement fee.

No State Licenses

While our trainees will be federally licensed, you will have to cover the costs of licensing them in states that you'd like them to operate in.
